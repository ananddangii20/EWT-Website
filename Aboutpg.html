What is EWT?

EWT is a student-led initiative at TCET that brings together creative, energetic, and compassionate students to organize events, volunteer, and make a positive impact on society.

From cultural festivals to social outreach programs, EWT stands for unity, creativity, and leadership. Through initiatives like Tiny Twirlers and Trishool, we combine fun with meaningful contributions.
