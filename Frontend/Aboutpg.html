WHAT IS EWT ?

EWT (Extension Work Team) is a student-led initiative at Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET), operating under the Scheme of Lifelong Learning and Extension guided by the University Grants Commission (UGC). It connects academic learning with social responsibility by engaging students in meaningful community outreach.

EWT works with underprivileged sections of society through educational and social initiatives aimed at improving quality of life. By promoting skill development, awareness, and participation, EWT encourages people-centric growth. Through extension education and field outreach activities, the team empowers individuals and communities to identify challenges, adopt innovative solutions, and create sustainable social impact through collective effort and responsible leadership.
